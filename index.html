Tampa Bay Lightning Trivia!

Press start to begin

Instructions:

You are given 60 seconds to anwser 5 questions by clicking on one of the answers. If an answer is incorrect you lose 10 seconds on the timer. Your score will be the number of correct answers added to your remaining time. You can enter your name on the Score Screen and if you would like you can clear the set of scores stored by clicking Clear Scores on the Score Screen.
